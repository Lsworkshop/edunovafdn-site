Posting Guidelines

We aim for a helpful, respectful, and trustworthy learning community.

‚úÖ What to do

  • Be specific: include your grade, goals, and constraints.
  • Share context: what have you tried and what worked?
  • Respect privacy: avoid posting sensitive personal data.
  • Be constructive: offer evidence, examples, and kindness.

üö´ What not to do

  • No harassment, hate, or discrimination.
  • No spam, misleading promotions, or suspicious links.
  • No sharing of copyrighted materials without permission.
  • No doxxing or requesting private info about others.

Moderation is maintained by volunteers. If you see problematic content, please report it.
