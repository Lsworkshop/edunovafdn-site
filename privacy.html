Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Effective date: 2026-02-08 Contact: team@edunovafdn.org

Contents

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Information 3. Cookies & Similar Technologies 4. Sharing & Disclosure 5. Data Security 6. Data Retention 7. Your Rights & Choices 8. Children’s Privacy 9. Changes to This Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and any details you submit through forms.

We may also collect basic technical information when you access the site, such as browser type, device information, and approximate location (e.g., country/region), mainly for security and analytics.

  • Account data (if you create an account): email, password hash, verification status.
  • Form submissions: any information you enter into registration or contact forms.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our services, including responding to requests and delivering content.
  • To maintain account security (e.g., email verification, session management).
  • To send service-related emails (verification, updates, important notices).

3. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences (such as language), and improve site performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features (e.g., login).

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., email delivery) strictly to operate our services.

We may also disclose information when required by law, or to protect the security and integrity of our services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information (where applicable).
  • Request deletion of your account data, subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications where available.

To make a request, contact us at team@edunovafdn.org.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date.
